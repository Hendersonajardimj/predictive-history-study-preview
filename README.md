# Predictive History Study Preview

## The world is not getting simpler. Your map has to get better.

Most people are trying to understand geopolitics, AI, religion, money, empire, media, and war as separate stories.

They are not separate stories.

Predictive History is an independent study-companion experiment for people who want a clearer way to follow the whole system: digest the arguments, inspect the source trail, compare the claims, and build a coherent worldview without drowning in tabs, transcripts, and headlines.

Start here:

https://predictive-history-web-production.up.railway.app/preview/human-cold-open-world-feels-too-complex-v1?utm_source=github_repo&utm_medium=public_readme&utm_campaign=ph_human_cold_open_preview&utm_content=github_public_repo_hook_v7

What the preview is testing:

- Whether a cold open can make the problem feel immediate in the first screen.
- Whether proof frames make the app feel like a real study tool instead of another AI summary.
- Whether a viewer wants the next map, digest, or source trail after seeing the concept.

The thesis is simple: people do not only want more content. They want a better map of the world.

This repo is not the official Predictive History channel. It is an owned public measurement surface for the study-companion experiment.
