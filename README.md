# Predictive History Study Preview

The world feels too complex to follow because the important stories are not separate.

Predictive History is a study companion for people who want to understand the world as a connected system: geopolitics, economics, technology, religion, empire, myth, and power moving together instead of as isolated headlines.

This preview tests a simple question:

Can a short cold open help a curious viewer understand why a larger Predictive History study app should exist?

Watch the preview:

https://predictive-history-web-production.up.railway.app/preview/human-cold-open-world-feels-too-complex-v1?utm_source=github_repo&utm_medium=public_readme&utm_campaign=ph_human_cold_open_preview&utm_content=github_public_repo_readme_v6

What to look for:

- Does the first viewport make the problem feel immediate?
- Do the proof frames make the app feel useful instead of generic?
- Would you want the next map, digest, or source trail?

This is not the official Predictive History channel. It is an independent study-companion experiment for readers and viewers who want a clearer way to work through the material.
